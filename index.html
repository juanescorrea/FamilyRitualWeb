Frequently Asked Questions

How does Family Ritual gamify routines?

Family Ritual turns morning and bedtime routines into a fun game with Agugu, using checklists, rewards, streak tracking, and collectible stickers. Kids follow clear steps, earn rewards, and build habits while parents enjoy calmer days.

What age group is Family Ritual designed for?

Family Ritual is designed for kids ages 2–8. Parents or guardians manage the account and supervise children while they complete routines.

Is Family Ritual free to use?

Family Ritual is free to start. Optional premium features are available through subscription. You can cancel anytime.

How do streaks and rewards work?

Kids earn rewards and build streaks by completing their daily routines. Progress is tracked day by day, motivating habit formation. These gamified elements are designed to motivate routine completion—they do not represent real-world value.

Why are consistent routines important for children?

Research shows that consistent bedtime routines improve sleep quality and emotion regulation in young children. The American Academy of Pediatrics recommends predictable routines like Brush, Book, Bed to ease bedtime stress and support healthy development.
